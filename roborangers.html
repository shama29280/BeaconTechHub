What is RoboRangers?

RoboRangers is a future-focused robotics program by Beacon Technology Hub that trains students in Arduino programming, sensor integration, automation systems, AI fundamentals, and real-world robotics projects. Our mission is to prepare students for Industry 4.0 and advanced technology careers.

What Students Learn

Arduino & Microcontrollers

Learn real hardware programming using Arduino boards and practical electronics.

Sensors & Automation

Build smart systems using motion sensors, rain sensors, ultrasonic modules, and more.

Robotics Design

Create robots from scratch including obstacle avoiding and line following robots.

AI & Future Tech

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and automation technologies.
